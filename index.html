Fight the Foot Clan

Instructions

The evil Shredder will give you a random number at the start of the game.
Each time you click on one of the 4 Ninja Turtles you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to Shredder's number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the Shedder's number.
The value of each Ninja Turtle is hidden from you until you click on one of them.
Each time the game starts, the values of each Ninja Turtle will change.
